How the fractional model works

A clean, controlled way to bring in executive-level help—without adding a full-time headcount before you’re ready.

Step 01

Clarify the gap

We start with a working session to understand where you’re stuck: cash clarity, delivery capacity, GTM motion, or leadership focus.

Step 02

Match with a fractional leader

We pull from our Job Seeker Program bench—operators who’ve signaled they are open to fractional work—and align them to your stage, sector, and goals.

Step 03

Run a focused sprint

You agree on scope, cadence, and outcomes. The work runs under the Offr Group umbrella, with a simple fractional fee structure.

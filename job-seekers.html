Job Seeker Program

A structured search engine
for your next role

Offr Group’s Job Seeker Program uses a four-layered outreach system to put your profile in front of investors, portfolio companies, active clients, and targeted hiring managers—all with a clear, aligned search strategy.

What we help you do

  • Clarify your executive narrative and target roles
  • Showcase your profile to curated investor & VC networks
  • Unlock fractional and full-time leadership opportunities
  • Reach decision-makers with warm, targeted outreach

How the Program Works

We use a four-layered approach to build momentum around your search and keep qualified opportunities flowing in—without you having to chase every posting.

Layer 1

Curated VC Network

Your executive profile is showcased to a selected network of investors and private-equity firms. Their portfolio companies have hiring managers who may be actively looking for talent like you.

Layer 2

Fractional Opportunities

Our optional fractional division identifies early-stage companies that can benefit from your GTM or growth expertise—ideal for portfolio-style work or interim leadership roles.

Layer 3

Active Offr Group Clients

We match you against roles from our active recruiting clients and introduce you when there is a strong match in scope, stage, and culture.

Layer 4

Targeted Hiring Managers

Together we build a list of specific hiring managers aligned to your identity as a leader—then we reach out directly on your behalf to generate new career conversations and offers.

Who the Program Is For

The program is designed for senior individual contributors, managers, and executives who want a more strategic, network-driven search instead of endlessly applying online.

  • Leaders in Sales, GTM, RevOps, CS, Marketing, or Product
  • Operators looking for VP, Head-of, or C-level roles
  • Executives open to fractional or portfolio-style work
  • Candidates who value high-touch support and accountability

How We Support You

This is not a “spray-and-pray” blast. We treat your search like a client engagement: structured, reported, and tuned as we go.

  • Story and positioning work for your executive profile
  • Target-company & target-manager list building
  • Done-for-you outreach into VCs, clients, and hiring leaders
  • Signal-based updates on who is engaging and where to double down
